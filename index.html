The founder

Preeti Raghunath, PhD

A former journalist, Preeti now teaches and researches communication technologies and policy at a leading UK university. Her expertise spans the global history of media and technology, digital media, AI, data governance, and emerging tech policy.

Preeti brings research and work experience in South and Southeast Asia, and the UK. She has consulted on multi-country research and policy projects, designed and facilitated design-thinking workshops, authored books and peer-reviewed papers, edited collections and long-form narrative work, covered election cycles, and reported human interest stories.

Why Alchemystical

Alchemystical lives in the meeting of things usually kept apart. We bring together two decades of experience across newsrooms, policy spaces, and academic research in one collaborative space. The best work happens when rigorous research, creative media, and policy thinking are not treated as silos.

Our perspective is shaped by a richly international background and ethos. We root each project in its own cultures and contexts rather than a single vantage point, attentive to plural histories and to the values our clients hold close. Rather than flattening difference into a template, we stay close to where each story and community actually stands. Preeti works alongside a trusted network of researchers and designers across the globe.

At the centre of everything is the lived experience of people and the dignity of their voice. That commitment is the lens we begin from, not a method we apply afterwards, and it runs through every piece of research, media, and policy thinking we take on.
